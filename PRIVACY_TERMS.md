# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 3, 2025

## Overview

LưuGọn is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

## What We Collect

- **Compressed Content:** Text or images you choose to share
- **IP Address:** For rate limiting and abuse prevention
- **Metadata:** File size, type, and creation timestamp

## What We DON'T Collect

- ❌ Personal identification information (name, email, etc.)
- ❌ Cookies or tracking data
- ❌ Usage analytics or telemetry
- ❌ Browser fingerprints

## Data Storage

- Your compressed content is stored in Supabase PostgreSQL
- Data can expire after a configurable period (default: never)
- We don't share data with third parties

## Security

- Data is stored in secure Supabase infrastructure
- All communications use HTTPS encryption
- File access is controlled by unique short codes

## Your Rights

- You can delete your content anytime (if delete feature available)
- You have the right to request data deletion
- We support anonymous usage - no account creation required

## Contact

For privacy concerns, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

## Changes

We reserve the right to update this policy. Material changes will be announced.
